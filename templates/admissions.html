Admissions

Welcome to the admissions portal of Calicut University Campus. We offer a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

Admission Process:

1. Check the eligibility criteria for your desired course.

2. Fill out the online application form available on the university website.

3. Submit the required documents and application fee.

4. Appear for entrance exams (if applicable).

5. Wait for the merit list and counseling process.

For more details, visit the official university website or contact the admissions office.
