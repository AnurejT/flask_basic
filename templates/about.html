About Us

Welcome to the University of Calicut, one of the premier institutions of higher education in Kerala, India. Established in 1968, the university is committed to academic excellence and research innovation.

Located in the scenic Malappuram district, the Calicut University Campus is home to various departments, research centers, and affiliated colleges, providing world-class education to thousands of students.

Our mission is to nurture knowledge, culture, and leadership through quality education and research-driven initiatives.
